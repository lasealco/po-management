# SRM Compliance and Document Control Specification

## Required Document Families

### Corporate
Examples:
- certificate of incorporation
- tax certificate
- company registration

Controls:
- versioning
- validity
- approval

### Financial / Payment
Examples:
- bank confirmation
- W-forms / tax forms
- payment details

Controls:
- restricted visibility
- approval

### Insurance
Examples:
- liability insurance
- cargo insurance
- warehouse insurance

Controls:
- expiry alerts
- mandatory thresholds

### Regulatory
Examples:
- broker licenses
- customs permits
- transport permits
- dangerous goods approvals

Controls:
- expiry and scope control

### Operational
Examples:
- SOPs
- service capability declarations
- contact matrices
- contingency plans

Controls:
- approval and review cycle

### Quality / Certification
Examples:
- ISO
- GDP
- TAPA
- food safety
- security certifications

Controls:
- validity and review

### Contracts
Examples:
- MSA summary
- addenda
- local annexes

Controls:
- versioning and validity

## Compliance Controls
- Document type templates with required/optional status by supplier category
- Expiry alerts with lead-time settings
- Approval workflow on upload or renewal
- Missing-document dashboard and request workflow
- Suspension or conditional status if critical docs expire
- Audit trail for upload, approval, rejection, replacement, and expiry
