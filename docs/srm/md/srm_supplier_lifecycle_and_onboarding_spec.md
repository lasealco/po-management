# SRM Supplier Lifecycle and Onboarding Specification

## Lifecycle Stages

### Prospective
Potential supplier identified.

### Applicant
Basic information submitted.

### Onboarding
Documents and setup in progress.

### Under Review
Commercial, compliance, and qualification decisions ongoing.

### Approved
Approved to be used but not yet operationally active.

### Active
Approved for live usage.

### Conditional
Active with restrictions or remediation actions.

### Suspended
Temporarily blocked.

### Terminated
No longer approved for use.

### Archived
Historical record only.

## Onboarding Checklist Elements
- Supplier legal details verified
- Tax and banking details collected
- Required contacts collected
- Service categories and geographies defined
- Insurance and licenses uploaded
- Sanctions and watchlist screening completed where applicable
- Qualification questionnaire completed
- Commercial terms captured at summary level
- Approval chain completed
- Activation decision logged

## Requalification Triggers
- Critical document expired or close to expiry
- Major incident or repeated service failure
- Long period of inactivity
- Change in ownership or legal entity
- New service category request
- Annual or periodic review due
