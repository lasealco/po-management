# SRM Performance, Risk, and KPI Specification

## Performance KPIs

### Service reliability
- On-time pickup
- On-time departure
- On-time arrival
- On-time delivery
- Milestone compliance

### Quality
- Claims ratio
- Damage ratio
- POD quality
- Document accuracy
- Billing accuracy

### Responsiveness
- Quote response time
- Issue response time
- Escalation responsiveness

### Capacity / continuity
- Capacity acceptance
- Rejection rate
- Blackout periods
- Surge handling

### Commercial
- Rate competitiveness
- Invoice dispute rate
- Cost variance
- Agreed-surcharge compliance

### Relationship / governance
- Review attendance
- Action-plan closure
- Responsiveness to audits

## Risk Dimensions

### Financial
- Late payment requests
- Insolvency indicators
- Financial document gaps

### Compliance
- Expired permits
- Sanctions/watchlist hits
- Missing insurance

### Operational
- Repeated delays
- Repeated service failures
- Claims
- Poor milestone data

### Capacity
- Overdependence
- Limited equipment
- Seasonal breakdowns

### Concentration
- Too much spend or too many shipments with one provider

### Geopolitical / Location
- Country instability
- Lane disruption
- Strike exposure

## Scorecard Design Rules
- Support weighted scorecards by supplier type and category.
- Keep metric definitions transparent and traceable to source data.
- Allow manual commentary but avoid unstructured-only scoring.
- Support period comparisons and trend charts.
- Link poor performance to remediation plans and review workflow.
